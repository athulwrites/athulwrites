Book Reviews

Notes on books that shaped my thinking.

Make It Stick — Brown, Roediger, McDaniel
Reviewed: Feb 2026

This book changed how I think about learning. The key idea is that struggling with recall strengthens memory more than passive rereading.

My biggest takeaway: learning should feel difficult. Easy studying is often fake learning.

Deep Work — Cal Newport
Reviewed: Jan 2026

Newport argues that focused, distraction-free work is a rare and valuable skill. I found the philosophy stronger than the practical tips.
